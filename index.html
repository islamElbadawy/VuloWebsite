Features

Cloud hosted

Vulo is a cloud-based solution hosted on world-class Cloud delivering to you a continuously highly available solution at a minimum uptime of 99.9%.

No-code Software

No-Code, Agile. Without any coding, Vulo delivers the full customizations needed to assure your business apps meet your needs.

Zero IT

Vulo requires zero IT infrastructure or personnel to support. Vulo saves you these costs and burden and lets you focus only on your business.

Rapid Delivery

Vulo is delivered in weeks including customizations and users training.
